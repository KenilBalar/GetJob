🏁 Who We Are?

Welcome to GetJob, your gateway to smarter hiring and job discovery.

At GetJob, we're passionate about simplifying the job market for everyone. Whether you're a job seeker searching for your next opportunity or an employer looking for the perfect candidate, we've got you covered.

We aim to:

  • 🧑‍💼 Connect talented individuals with great companies
  • 💼 Make job posting and job searching easy and efficient
  • 🛠️ Help students and developers like us build and learn real-world projects
This site is a learning project built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and serves as a starting point to explore how real job portals work, both in design and functionality
